Rameswaram

Rameswaram, a sacred island, is home to the renowned Ramanathaswamy Temple and the historic Ram Setu. It captivates visitors with its spiritual essence, stunning Pamban Bridge, and serene beaches.

Rameswaram, a sacred island, enchants visitors with the majestic Ramanathaswamy Temple, the legendary Ram Setu, and its serene coastal beauty.

A sacred town steeped in mythology and devotion, Rameswaram is a spiritual gem of Tamil Nadu. Home to the revered Ramanathaswamy Temple, the mystical Ram Setu, and tranquil shores, it offers a journey of faith and history. From the breathtaking Pamban Bridge to the serene Agni Theertham, Rameswaram seamlessly blends divine heritage with natural beauty, making it a must-visit destination for pilgrims and travelers alike.

With deep spiritual significance and a serene coastal charm, Rameswaram welcomes pilgrims and travelers alike. It’s not just about temples and legends—Rameswaram offers much more. Majestic sea bridges, sacred theerthams, pristine beaches, and a rich cultural legacy make it a destination of faith and wonder. No wonder Rameswaram is often called the'Varanasi of the South'..

Explore Rameswaram's Wonders

Ramanathaswamy Temple

One of the most sacred temples in India, this shrine is known for its long corridors, towering gopurams, and spiritual significance.

Dhanushkodi

A ghost town with stunning coastal views, Dhanushkodi holds mythological and historical significance as the starting point of Ram Setu.

Pamban Bridge

India’s first sea bridge, this architectural marvel connects Rameswaram to the mainland and offers breathtaking ocean views.

Agni Theertham

A sacred beach where pilgrims take a holy dip before visiting the Ramanathaswamy Temple, believed to cleanse sins.

Gandhamadhana Parvatham

A revered hilltop with a small shrine that houses Lord Rama’s footprint, offering a panoramic view of Rameswaram.

Kothandaramaswamy Temple

A peaceful temple near Dhanushkodi, associated with Lord Rama’s meeting with Vibhishana in the Ramayana.
