Ooty- The Queen of Hill Stations

A paradise for travelers, Ooty is home to the serene Ooty Lake, vibrant botanical gardens, and the breathtaking Doddabetta Peak.

Udhagamandalam, popularly known as Ooty, is a hill station that will surely mesmerize you with its beguiling beauty.

A chain of misty hills spread across a lush green carpet – with plenty to see, enjoy and experience. Ooty is much more than a visitor’s paradise. It lends a piece of itself to its guests that they will be thrilled to take back. One of the most beautiful hill stations in India, Ooty nestles in the Nilgiri district of Tamil Nadu.

With a cool and soothing climate almost all year round, Ooty welcomes tourists from far and wide. It’s not just about the mountains and hills, Ooty offers much more to discerning travellers. Lakes, gardens, parks, waterfalls, and simply amazing accommodation options. No wonder Ooty is called the 'Switzerland of India'.

Explore Ooty’s Wonders

Government Botanical Garden, Ooty

The Government Botanical Garden in Ooty, spread over 55 acres, is a mesmerizing tourist site that has been attracting visitors for years.

Ooty Lake

The picturesque Ooty Lake, also known as the Ooty boat house, is a much sought-after tourist attraction located in the heart of the town.

Pine Forest

A journey of just 15 minutes from Ooty town centre will take you to the famed Pine Tree Forests of Ooty, a scenic and peaceful getaway.

Doddabetta Peak

Doddabetta, the highest peak in the Nilgiri Hills, offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape, making it a must-visit spot.

Ooty Rose Garden

The Ooty Rose Garden, home to thousands of varieties of roses, is a paradise for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Tea Museum

Discover the rich history of tea production in Ooty at the Tea Museum, where you can learn about tea processing and sample fresh brews.
